Want to learn to draw?
If you want to become an professional artist, enhance your abilities, learn to draw but don't know where to start, or don't know where to start to start learning to draw, you should look up at Art Fundamentals. Art Fundamentals cover the actual topics you need to master in order to become an experienced artist who is also able to teach art. This page section of the website is intended to give a tiny guide of where to start.
Shape & form
Description
Shape & form regards the knowledge and skill to be able to build any object from reality into a canvas/paper by breaking it into simple shapes.
Resources

Drawabox is an extensive free online shape/form and perspective exercise-based course. It won't teach you to draw objects, animals, humans, etc... but do will teach you all you need to draw everything out from basic/complex shapes.
